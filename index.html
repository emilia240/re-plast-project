Environment

Plastic and humans

People consume around 5 grams of plastic weekly, equivalent to a credit card.

Plastic and reuse

Over 400 million tons of plastic are produced yearly, and only 9% is recycled.

Plastic and the ocean

About 11 million tons of plastic enter the oceans annually, projected to triple by 2040 if no action is taken.
